# Party Supplies La Poblanita 2 — Owner Walkthrough (Oscar)

A start-to-finish checklist for running an order, plus how every setting works.

---

## 1. Create a new order — top to bottom

Click **+ New Order**.

### Dates
- [ ] **Today's date** — auto-filled.
- [ ] **Event Name** — e.g. "Maria's Quinceañera". Shows on the payment link & paperwork. (Optional but recommended — it auto-fills the payment note.)
- [ ] **Event Start Date** — ⚠️ warns if you pick a date in the past.
- [ ] **Delivery Time**.
- [ ] **Pickup Date** — auto-fills to the next day (change if needed).
- [ ] **Pickup Time** — auto-fills to the delivery time (change if needed).

### Customer
- [ ] **First Name** (required), **Last Name**.
- [ ] **Phone** — ⚠️ warns if it's not 10 digits (or 11 with a leading 1). The +1 is optional.
- [ ] **Email** — needed to email the signing link / receipt.
- [ ] **Find address as I type** — check this to get Google address suggestions (auto-fills City/State/ZIP). Leave unchecked to type it manually.
- [ ] **Address / Apt / City / State / ZIP**.

### Setup
- [ ] **Surface** — Grass / Cement / Dirt / House / Park, plus **Stairs (extra charge)**.
- [ ] **Property type** — **Private** or **Public**. Public (e.g. a park) **skips the photo/video consent clause** on the contract. *Checking the "Park" surface auto-switches this to Public.*

### Items — **three ways to price** (pick whatever fits the job)
Each catalog row has: **Qty**, variation **checkboxes**, **$ Amount**, and a **Notes** line. Use **+ Add [item]** to add a second row of the same item (e.g. two different table types).

1. **Itemize (line-by-line pricing)** — put the qty, check the variations, and enter the **$ Amount for that line**. Repeat per item. Best when you price each item.
2. **Multiple checkboxes, no per-item price** — if you're *not* itemizing, just set the qty and **check the variations** to record what's going out (e.g. Jumpers: Regular + Big Slide + Obstacles), and leave the amounts blank. Then put the price in one place (see #3 or the deposit/total).
3. **"Full Amount" as an Other item** — scroll to **Other → + Add other**, type **"Full Amount"** (or "Bundle", "Package deal", etc.) as the description and put the **bundled price** there. Use this when the customer gets one all-in price instead of itemized.
   > Tip: #2 and #3 work great together — checkboxes record *what's going out*, and "Full Amount" records *what they pay*.

### Delivery & miles
- [ ] **Delivery fee** — adds to the total.
- [ ] **Miles** — for your reference.

### Payment
- [ ] **Method** — Zelle / Square / Cash.
- [ ] **Payment note** — auto-fills **"For {Event} on {date}"**. Use it as-is, add to it, or clear and write your own. **↺ Reset to default** brings the template back. Max **175 characters** (counter shown) — it appears on the customer's Square checkout, so keep it short.

### Totals (right side)
- [ ] **Delivery** → **Subtotal** (items + delivery) → **Tax** (auto from your rate, or override) → **Total**.
- [ ] **Deposit** — defaults to **50%**, override if needed.
- [ ] **Balance** — auto (total − deposit).

### Driver's license
- [ ] Snap or upload the customer's **DL photo** (auto-deletes 30 days after the event).

- [ ] **Save Draft.**

## 2. Send to the customer (signing)

On the order page → **Send signing link to customer**:
- [ ] **Email** it (add a note / CC if you want), **Text** it, **Share**, or **Copy link**.
- The customer reviews the order, agrees to the waiver, **signs**, and can upload their **DL photo**.
- [ ] **License photo blurry?** After signing, use **Ask customer to retake / Send DL retake** to email or text them a retake link (signature stays locked).

---

## 3. Collect payment (Square)

### Deposit
- [ ] **Create deposit link** (Payment section) → **Text / Share** it or it shows in the signing flow.
- When the customer pays, the order **auto-marks "Deposit Paid · via Square"** — no manual step.
- Checkout shows **"Poblanita 2 · {Event} · Deposit · {your note}"** so they know what it's for.

### Balance / amount owed (incl. pay-in-full on delivery)
- [ ] **Collect $X** block → **Create payment link** → **Text / Share**.
- The amount is **whatever's still owed**: the remaining balance, or the **full total if no deposit was paid** (customer pays everything on delivery).
- Paying it **settles the order automatically**.

### Cash / other
- [ ] Use **Mark deposit paid / Mark balance paid** by hand. (Square payments mark themselves.)

---

## 4. Delivery crew

Each order has a **crew job ticket link** (`/job/...`) — send it to the driver. It shows:
- [ ] **When**, **where** (with **Open in Maps**), **what to deliver** (items + variations + notes), surface & stairs.
- [ ] **Pay by card** — crew taps **Card payment link ($X)**, then **texts it** to the customer or **Show QR** for them to scan & pay on the spot. Auto-marks paid.
- [ ] **Text owner: payment collected** — for cash; tells the office, who marks it paid.
> The job ticket hides the DL, signature, contract, and itemized pricing — crew only sees what they need.

## 5. Marketing — setup photos, video, reviews & content

> Everything below the purple **MARKETING** banner is for social media — **not** part of the rental contract. The banner shows the consent status: **🏞 public property** (no consent needed), **✅ customer approved social use**, or **⚠️ no consent — ask before posting**.

### a) Capture on-site (crew)
- [ ] In **Setup photos**, use the **Crew upload link** — **Copy** it and text it to the crew, or **email a team member**. They open it and upload **setup photos** + a short **walkthrough video** (≤1 min) from the event.
- [ ] You can also add photos/video yourself right there.

### b) Send the best shots to the customer (a few days after the event)
- [ ] Look through the uploaded photos/videos and **select** your best.
- [ ] **✉️ Send to customer + review** — emails them the selected photos & video **with your Google review link** (or **Share / Text** the gallery). It shows "Last sent…" so you know it went.
- This is the **review funnel**: the happy customer sees their event photos and a one-tap **"Leave a review."** (The review link comes from **Settings → Google review link**.)

### c) Send content to your producer
- [ ] In **Content Creation**, **select** which photos & videos go to your content creator, then **Send** — it goes to the producer email(s) set in **Settings**.
- [ ] Videos **auto-delete 20 days** after upload.

---

## 6. After the event

On the order page:
- [ ] **Mark delivered**, **Mark picked up**, **Mark completed**.
- [ ] **Send receipt** (email, with optional CC/BCC/note).
- [ ] **Archive** when done; **Delete** to permanently remove (also deletes uploaded files).
- DL photos **auto-delete 30 days after the event**.

Also: **Orders** list (search/filter) and **Calendar** view (see what's going out each day) — both show the customer name + event.

---

## 7. Settings — what each one does

Open **Settings** (top nav).

- **Sales tax rate** — your % (e.g. 9.5). Auto-applies tax to every order; you can still override tax on a single order.
- **Driver's license** — toggle **"Require a DL photo before the customer can sign."** DL photos auto-delete 30 days after the event.
- **Producer email (content)** — where **"Send to producer"** emails go (setup photos + videos for editing). Add more than one.
- **Video release text** — what the customer agrees to before recording a video testimonial.
- **Photo/video consent (signing)** — the optional consent checkbox shown when signing (permission for setup photos + walkthrough video). Doesn't block signing. Supports markdown (Preview tab).
- **Staff / team** — add crew (name, email, phone) so you can email/text the job link to a team member from a dropdown.
- **Google review link** — paste your Google review URL; powers the "Leave a review" button on the event-photo page.
- **Waiver text** — what customers read and agree to before signing. Editing it bumps the version; already-signed orders keep the exact text they agreed to.
- **Change password** — update your sign-in password (needs the current one).

## Quick pricing cheat-sheet

| Situation | What to do |
|---|---|
| Price each item | **Itemize** — qty + checkboxes + $ Amount per line |
| Just recording what's going out, no per-item price | **Check the variations**, set qty, leave amounts blank |
| One bundled/package price | **Other → "Full Amount"** with the total price |
| Mix | Checkboxes for *what's going out* + "Full Amount" for *what they pay* |
